# WhatsApp Sales English Manual

这份手册用于和海外客户在 WhatsApp 上沟通。句子尽量短、清楚、礼貌，适合直接复制后替换具体信息。

## 1. 开场和跟进

### 客户刚联系你

Thanks for getting in touch. Could you please share more details about your requirements?

谢谢你联系我。请发我更多需求细节。

What specific products are you looking for?

你正在找哪类产品？

Could you please send me a picture or drawing for reference?

请发我图片或图纸作为参考。

### 跟进客户

May I ask if there have been any updates?

请问目前有更新吗？

Just checking if you have had time to review the quotation.

想确认一下你是否已经看过报价。

Please let me know if you have any questions.

如果有任何问题，请告诉我。

## 2. 确认需求

Could you please confirm the quantity you need?

请确认你需要的数量。

Could you please confirm the color and finish?

请确认颜色和表面效果。

Do you need the cap only, or the cap with bottle and pump together?

你只需要盖子，还是盖子、瓶子和泵头一起需要？

Do you need a shiny finish or a matte finish?

你需要亮面还是哑光？

Please send us the swatch, and we will match the color according to it.

请把色板寄给我们，我们会按色板配色。

## 3. 报价

Please find our quotation below.

以下是我们的报价。

The EXW unit price is USD __ per piece.

EXW 单价是每个 __ 美元。

The price is valid for 10 days.

此价格有效期为 10 天。

The price depends on the final quantity and finish.

价格取决于最终数量和表面效果。

The price will be a little higher because the quantity is lower.

因为数量较少，价格会稍微高一点。

Air freight is faster, but the cost is higher.

空运更快，但费用更高。

## 4. 样品

We can make samples first for your confirmation.

我们可以先做样品给你确认。

The sample lead time is about __ days.

样品交期大约 __ 天。

Please confirm the sample before we start mass production.

大货生产前请先确认样品。

If you need changes, please mark them clearly on the picture.

如果需要修改，请在图片上清楚标出来。

## 5. 模具

This model requires a new mold.

这个款式需要开新模具。

The mold cost is USD __.

模具费是 __ 美元。

The mold cost needs to be paid before mold making.

模具费需要在开模前支付。

Mold making will take about __ days.

开模大约需要 __ 天。

## 6. 生产和进度

The goods are currently in production.

货物目前正在生产中。

It should be ready for shipment next week.

预计下周可以发货。

We can start mass production after you confirm the sample.

你确认样品后，我们可以开始大货生产。

I will check with our factory and update you today.

我会跟工厂确认，今天给你更新。

I understand this is urgent. I will push the factory and update you as soon as possible.

我理解这很紧急。我会催工厂并尽快更新你。

Reprocessing will take about __ days.

返工大约需要 __ 天。

## 7. 付款和单据

Please check the PI and let me know if you have any questions.

请检查 PI，如有任何问题请告诉我。

Please arrange the balance payment before shipment.

请在发货前安排尾款。

Please send me the payment receipt after payment.

付款后请把付款凭证发给我。

We have received your payment. Thank you.

我们已收到你的付款，谢谢。

I will send you the invoice and packing list today.

我今天会把发票和装箱单发给你。

We can only ship the goods after our finance department completes the review.

财务审核完成后，我们才能发货。

## 8. 物流和清关

The goods are ready for shipment.

货物已经可以发货。

The goods have arrived at the freight forwarder's warehouse.

货物已经到达货代仓库。

Please ask your freight forwarder to contact us.

请让你的货代联系我们。

I will send you the tracking number once it is available.

物流单号出来后我会发给你。

Your forwarder needs to handle customs clearance.

你的货代需要处理清关。

Based on recent rail shipping delays, I do not suggest choosing rail shipping at the moment.

根据最近铁路运输延误情况，我目前不建议选择铁路运输。

我理解你的担心。

我理解这很紧急。

让我确认当前状态并尽快回复你。

我会催工厂并尽快更新你。

Sorry for the late reply.

抱歉回复晚了。

感谢你的耐心和理解。

## 10. 客户常问句和推荐回复

### What is the price?

The unit price depends on the quantity and finish. Could you please confirm the quantity you need?

### Can you send me the quote?

Sure. I will prepare the quotation and send it to you soon.

### How long will it take?

The lead time is about __ days after sample confirmation and deposit payment.

### Any update?

Let me check with our factory and update you today.

### Where is the tracking number?

The tracking number is not available yet. I will send it to you once it is available.

### Can we change the color?

Yes, we can check it. Please send us the color swatch or Pantone number for reference.

### Can you make samples first?

Yes, we can make samples first for your confirmation.

### We will pay the balance today.

Thank you. Please send me the payment receipt after payment, so we can arrange shipment.
